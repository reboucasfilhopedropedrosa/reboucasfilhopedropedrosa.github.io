Prof. Pedro Pedrosa Rebouças Filho

Ph.D. (2013) and Master (2009) in Teleinformatics Engineering from the Federal University of Ceará, Graduated in Industrial Mechatronics from the Federal Institute of Education, Science, and Technology of Ceará (2007). He did a post-doctoral internship at the University of Porto (FEUP) in Portugal, funded by CNPq during 2015 and 2016, in the area of medical diagnosis aided by images using Applied Computational Intelligence with Digital Image Processing methods.

Experience as a professor: He is currently an Associate Professor at the Federal Institute of Education, Science, and Technology of Ceará (IFCE), where he teaches master’s level courses in Computer Science and undergraduate courses in Mechatronics Engineering and Computer Engineering. He works as a professor for graduate programs at the Federal University of Ceará (UFC) as a permanent member at PPGETI-UFC (Score 6 at CAPES) and PPGEE-UFC (Score 5 at CAPES), in the Master’s and Ph.D. programs in Teleinformatics Engineering and Engineering Electrical, respectively.

Experience in management positions: He was the research coordinator at IFCE Maracanaú from 2013 to 2014, participating and being ahead of the proposal for the master’s degrees in Renewable Energy and Computer Science in 2013. He also worked as coordinator of the master’s degree in Renewable Energy from 2014 to 2015 and as coordinator of the master’s degree in Computer Science from 2016 to 2020.

Experience as a researcher: He is currently a researcher and leader of the research group registered at CNPq called Laboratory of Image Processing, Signals and Applied Computing (LAPISCO). LAPISCO is more than a physical space and is where he conducts his academic research and technological development since 2012 within IFCE (http://lapisco.ifce.edu.br). He has experience in the Computer System area, working on lines of research such as Applied Computing, Intelligent Systems, and 3D Visualization and Processing. He has a deep interest in Pattern Recognition, Artificial Intelligence, Signal Processing and Analysis, Image Processing and Analysis, Mobile and Industrial Robotics, Automation, and Embedded Systems. He has more than 100 articles published in journals and more than 100 articles published at international and national conferences. He is a reviewer in approximately 15 international journals and a guest editor of Special Issues in them.
